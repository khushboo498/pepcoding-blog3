Why should you choose Pepcoding?

Pepcoding is your one-way drive to get ahead of the curve from other coders. It is a platform where aspiring coders get a chance to develop their understanding while mastering their coding skills. You get to study from zealous teaching staff who are not only industry experts but also most dedicated to the art of teaching. At the same time, healthy competition with peers makes you strive to always do better. In this article you would get to know why Pepcoding is the go-to choice of millions of aspiring Indian coders and should be yours too.

The Content

To begin with, its content on data structures and algorithms is unparalleled. The vast range of study material available for free on the website is being used by lakhs of coders to clarify and strengthen their concepts. It provides you over 600 leetcode type problems. And its question bank on codechef and codeforces can turn you into a wizard of coding.

Placement Programs

What’s more, the organization ensures that their students land their desired stable jobs in renowned, well-paying corporations. There is a module to train you rigorously for interviews in top corporations, making you ace your interviews in companies such as FAANG.

Two popular courses are First job program (FJP) and Job Switch Program (JSP). FJP is designed for 3rd/4th year college students preparing for their first job. While JSP is targeted towards working professionals planning to switch to better corporations. Starting from freelance jobs and internship, the courses give its students ample opportunity to gain industry experience which, as we know, is crucial in today’s competitive work climate.
